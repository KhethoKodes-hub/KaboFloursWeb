Need Help?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and ordering process

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing custom orders at least 7 days in advance. For wedding cakes or large events, 2-3 weeks' notice is ideal to ensure we can accommodate your request. For everyday bakes, same-day orders are often possible if placed before 10 AM.

Do you offer gluten-free or sugar-free options?

Yes! We offer both gluten-free and sugar-free options for most of our products. Please mention your dietary requirements when placing your order, and we'll be happy to accommodate your needs. Please note that while we take precautions, our kitchen is not entirely gluten-free.

What is your delivery area and cost?

We deliver throughout Postmasburg and surrounding areas. Delivery starts at R30, with free delivery for orders over R300. For locations outside our standard delivery zone, additional charges may apply. Contact us for specific delivery quotes to your area.

Can I customize the flavors in my order?

Absolutely! We love creating custom flavor combinations. During your consultation, we'll discuss your preferences and suggest flavors that work well together. We have a wide range of options from classic vanilla and chocolate to more unique flavors like red velvet, lemon lavender, and caramel crunch.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, EFT payments, and mobile payments. For custom orders, we require a 50% deposit to secure your booking, with the balance due upon delivery or pickup. For everyday bakes, payment is due at the time of order or pickup.
