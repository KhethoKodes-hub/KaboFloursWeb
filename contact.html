Common Questions

Contact FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and placing orders

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For messages received outside of business hours, we'll respond first thing the next business day. For urgent matters, calling us directly is always the fastest option.

Do you offer delivery outside of Postmasburg?

Yes, we offer delivery to surrounding areas with additional delivery fees based on distance. Contact us with your specific location for a delivery quote. For larger orders or events, we can often accommodate deliveries to more distant locations.

Can I place an order for same-day pickup?

For our everyday bakes, same-day orders are often possible if placed before 10 AM. For custom cakes and specialty orders, we typically require at least 24-48 hours notice to ensure we can create something perfect for you. Always call ahead to check same-day availability.

What information should I include when placing a custom order?

For custom orders, please include: the date of your event, number of guests, preferred flavors, any dietary restrictions, your budget range, and any inspiration photos or design ideas you have. The more details you can provide, the better we can bring your vision to life!

Do you have a catalog of your past work I can browse?

Yes! You can view our gallery on our website or visit our social media pages to see examples of our work. We're constantly updating with new creations. If you have something specific in mind, feel free to share reference images with us during your consultation.
